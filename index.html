Who We Are

Grassroots Reality meets Progressive Vision

H.N. Vyas Projects Pvt. Ltd. (formerly AEGIS Consultancy) is a Next-Gen consultancy founded by two visionary entrepreneurs with over 15 years of experience in project design, procurement, execution, and commissioning.

With feet on the ground and eyes on the horizon, we combine grassroots reality with developmental aspirations of citizens across villages, talukas, and districts of India.

Through Corporate Social Responsibility, we provide expert, transparent, end-to-end project implementation services — ensuring every rupee is invested for intended social welfare.

🏛️ Management

Led by Hema Vyas, Director — MBA HR, an entrepreneur with 15 years of rich experience in People and Project Management.

👥 Our Team

35 committed professionals, organized department-wise and dedicated to each project with precision and accountability.

🌐 Our Reach

Operating across Gujarat, Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Delhi NCR, and Diu Daman — with 20+ branch locations pan-India.

🤝 Our Approach

Expert, transparent and end-to-end project implementation services connecting corporate intent with community-level outcomes.
